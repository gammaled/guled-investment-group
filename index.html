WHAT WE OFFER:

Open-Ended Investment Fund

What we are offering is to invest your money in our open-ended investment fund, which is a fund where you can sell your shares ANYTIME you want in the market, by our talented investment managers.

Performance Based

Our investment fund is performance based which means we ONLY take profit if the fund has gained profit. Any profits at 4%, you will keep and anything below that, we will cover the difference. For profits higher than 4%, we take a commission of 20%.

We Cover Losses

Any losses upto 4% are ENTIRELY COVERED by us! And we cover HALF of any losses more than 4%.

We are fully invested too!

Most of our corporate assets have been invested in the fund itself. We fully believe in what we do and what other way to show it than to invest alongside you. We will be investing for ourselves AND yourself. We will be in this together!
